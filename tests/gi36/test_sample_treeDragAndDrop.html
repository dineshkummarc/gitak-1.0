Test Drag and drop command "dragJsxToJsx". Do drag and drop from tree to block.
Drag and drop from Tree to Block
include testSetup.html  
jsxopen ${GIPATH}/shell.html?jsxapppath=${SAMPLESPATH}/sample-treeDragAndDrop&jsxlt=true  
waitForElementPresent JsxTreeName=treeBreedSelection 5000
assertElementPresent JsxTreeName=treeBreedSelection  
assertElementPresent JsxTreeItemId=treeBreedSelection,c  
clickJsxTreeItem JsxTreeItemId=treeBreedSelection,c  
dragJsxToJsx JsxTreeItemId=treeBreedSelection,c JsxName=txtBreed
assertJsxValueEqual txtBreed Poodle
dragJsxToJsx JsxTreeItemText=treeBreedSelection,*Mutt* JsxName=blkRightPane
assertJsxValueEqual txtBreed Mutt
typeJsxTextbox JsxTextboxName=txtBreed German Shepherd
typeJsxTextbox JsxTextboxName=txtSize Medium
click JsxButtonName=btnAddItem
